About Qbit Infinity Ceramics

A Diversified Enterprise Driving Sustainable Progress

Qbit Infinity Ceramics Private Limited is a leading conglomerate based in Nagaur, Rajasthan. We operate across a diverse portfolio encompassing mining, processing of industrial minerals, ceramics manufacturing, utility solar energy, and international trade.

Backed by a strong foundation of 6 group companies, we deliver premium ceramic raw materials to global markets and build state-of-the-art solar installations in India. Our strategic operations are driven by excellence, technology integration, and environmental responsibility.

Advanced Processing

Modern plant infrastructure ensures precise sizing, grading, and purification of industrial minerals.

Clean Energy Focused

Substantial investments in grid-aligned and captive commercial solar plants across Rajasthan.

Our Legacy & Values

From the heart of Rajasthan's mineral belt, we serve global needs. Our commitment lies in sustainable mining, quality assurance, and clean energy development.

About QMM

Qbit Mines and Minerals is a premier mining and mineral processing division of Qbit Infinity Group. The company owns and operates rich open-cast mines in the key mineral corridor of Jayal, Nagaur, Rajasthan. We specialize in the extraction, sorting, processing, and delivery of high-purity industrial and ceramic raw materials.

Our state-of-the-art mining operations are backed by modern heavy earthmoving machinery, strict quality grading, and environment-friendly mining practices. We are dedicated to ensuring a consistent and reliable supply of high-grade raw materials to ceramics and chemical industries across India and global markets.

Corporate Vision

"To be a trusted and sustainable partner in industrial mineral sourcing by integrating advanced environment-friendly mining technology with strict quality control frameworks."

Mining Practices & Values

  • Sustainable open-cast mining methods.
  • Environment-friendly quarrying and excavation.
  • Strict moisture and size grading parameters.
  • Safe labor standards and site safety frameworks.
  • Land backfilling and ecological restoration.

Core Operations & Minerals

Mining & Resource Extraction
  • Open-cast clay quarrying operations in Rajasthan
  • Raw ball clay and China clay excavation
  • Geological mapping and resource estimation
  • Environmental impact compliance & safety plans
Mineral Processing & Grading
  • High-efficiency crushing, screening, and sizing
  • Sieve grading for specific mesh sizing parameters
  • Purification and washing (levigation) controls
  • Moisture content control and bulk packaging
Industrial Raw Material Sourcing
  • Bulk raw Ball Clay supplies for tiles body matrix
  • Potash Feldspar and Soda Feldspar sourcing
  • High-purity crystalline Silica & Quartz powders
  • Pan-India logistics and port delivery setups

About Dharti Mines

Dharti Mines is a key mining enterprise under the Qbit Infinity Group, specializing in the extraction and supply of high-grade industrial minerals and ceramics raw materials. Our primary focus is on sustainable and eco-sensitive excavation, advanced sizing, and prompt supply of industrial silica, clay, and related minerals to manufacturing industries across the country.
