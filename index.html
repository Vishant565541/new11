About Qbit Infinity Ceramics

A Diversified Enterprise Driving Sustainable Progress

Qbit Infinity Ceramics Private Limited is a leading conglomerate based in Nagaur, Rajasthan. We operate across a diverse portfolio encompassing mining, processing of industrial minerals, ceramics manufacturing, utility solar energy, and international trade.

Backed by a strong foundation of 6 group companies, we deliver premium ceramic raw materials to global markets and build state-of-the-art solar installations in India. Our strategic operations are driven by excellence, technology integration, and environmental responsibility.

Advanced Processing

Modern plant infrastructure ensures precise sizing, grading, and purification of industrial minerals.

Clean Energy Focused

Substantial investments in grid-aligned and captive commercial solar plants across Rajasthan.

Our Legacy & Values

From the heart of Rajasthan's mineral belt, we serve global needs. Our commitment lies in sustainable mining, quality assurance, and clean energy development.

What We Offer

Core Products & Divisions

⛏️ Mining & Minerals

Supplying raw and processed minerals of high mineral purity to domestic and international ceramic industries.

  • Ceramic Minerals: High-plasticity Ball Clay, White China Clay, Potash, and Soda Feldspar for body structures.
  • Industrial Minerals: High-grade Silica, Quartz powder, Bentonite, and Pyrophyllite for refractories and foundries.
  • Mines & Quarrying: Sustainable pit operations utilizing eco-sensitive extraction procedures and backfilling.

☀️ Solar Energy

Pioneering clean energy transitions through utility infrastructure development and industrial rooftop installations.

  • Solar Power Projects: End-to-end solar farm EPC including ground mapping, mounting structure, grid sync, and cabling.
  • Green Energy Solutions: Captive solar plant deployment helping manufacturing units reduce electrical bills and offset carbon footprints.
  • Discom Solar Projects: Developing power generation stations feeding green energy directly into central state DISCOM grids.
